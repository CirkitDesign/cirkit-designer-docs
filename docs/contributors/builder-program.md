# Component Builder Program

Join a select group of founding contributors who get exclusive access to our Component Editor to build simulatable components for the Cirkit Designer ecosystem.

## What is a Component Builder?

Component Builders are approved community members who help expand Cirkit's library of simulatable parts. As a builder, you'll have exclusive access to our powerful Component Editor with AI assistance—the same premium tools our subscribers use.

## What You Get

- **Exclusive Component Editor access**: Includes AI-powered assistance for component creation
- **Bounty opportunities**: Earn money by building high-priority components with cash rewards
- **Featured recognition**: Your work gets highlighted in our newsletter (100k+ subscribers) and showcased on our homepage
- **Future revenue sharing**: Earn ongoing income as your components get used across the platform (coming soon)
- **Early contributor status**: Be recognized as one of the founding builders who helped shape Cirkit

## What We're Looking For

We're selecting builders who can write high-quality code and documentation. Ideal candidates have:

- Experience with electronics, circuit simulation, web development (HTML and TypeScript ideally)
- Ability to write clean, well-documented code
- Strong technical writing skills for component documentation and examples
- Commitment to building components that benefit the entire community
- Interest in helping establish quality standards for the ecosystem

## Your Responsibilities

As an approved Component Builder, you're building components that will be used by hundreds of thousands of users—they need to work flawlessly:

- Create well-tested, documented simulation components that work reliably across all use cases
- Follow our strict submission guidelines and quality standards
- Thoroughly test components in various circuit contexts before submission
- Help establish best practices for the growing builder community

## How It Works

1. **Apply**: Fill out our application form with your background and relevant experience
2. **Review**: We'll review applications and select builders who align with our goals
3. **Approval**: Approved builders get immediate access to the Component Editor
4. **Build**: Start creating components with AI assistance and comprehensive documentation
5. **Submit**: Submit your components following our guidelines
6. **Payment**: If you've built a bountied component, you'll receive the cash bounty via PayPal or Venmo upon approval
7. **Recognition**: Accepted components get featured in our mailing list and on our homepage, and you get recognized as the creator
