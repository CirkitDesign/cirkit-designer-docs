Become a founding contributor to Cirkit Designer by building simulatable components that power the ecosystem. We’re entering a community-driven phase—this is your chance to help build Cirkit with us.

Our vision is to make Cirkit the most powerful simulation platform in the world, where thousands of components can be simulated. Every part you contribute moves us closer to that goal.

## How You Can Contribute

- Apply to Become a Component Builder  
  Get exclusive access to the Component Editor with powerful AI tools that assist with building simulatable components.  
  👉 [Apply to Become a Builder](https://app.cirkitdesigner.com/component-builder-registration)

- Earn Rewards with Bounties  
  Pick high-priority components, build them, and get paid when accepted.  
  👉 [View Current Bounties](https://app.cirkitdesigner.com/component-bounties)

## Why Contribute

- **Earn money**: Build bountied components and get paid when accepted. Long-term, revenue sharing from component usage means your earnings scale with Cirkit's growth—potentially far exceeding the initial bounty.
- **Get massive visibility**: Featured component builders get highlighted in our newsletter (100k+ subscribers) and showcased on our homepage (thousands of daily visitors).
- **Build for hundreds of thousands of users**: Your components will be used by Cirkit's massive user base, giving your work real-world impact at scale.
- **Be part of something big**: We're only accepting the best submission for each component. Get in early as a founding contributor to what could become the industry standard simulation platform.
- **Build with cutting-edge tools**: AI-assisted editor, comprehensive APIs, and example components make development faster and more accessible.
