# Component Bounties

Earn cash rewards by building high-priority simulation components for the Cirkit Designer ecosystem. Each bounty is awarded to the first accepted submission for that specific component that meets our quality standards.

## How Bounty Submissions Work

1. **Browse active bounties** on our [bounty board](https://app.cirkitdesigner.com/component-bounties) to see which components have cash rewards
2. **Build your component** using the Component Editor ([apply to become a Component Builder](https://app.cirkitdesigner.com/component-builder-registration) for exclusive access)
3. **Create an example project** that demonstrates your component working correctly
4. **Submit your claim** by expanding the bounty dropdown and clicking "Claim" on the [bounty board](https://app.cirkitdesigner.com/component-bounties)
5. **We review** your submission and either accept, reject, or request changes via email
6. **Get paid** - we'll reach out via email to confirm your preferred payment method (PayPal or Venmo) once your submission is accepted


## Bounty Rules

- **One winner per bounty**: Only the first accepted submission receives the reward
- **Quality standards apply**: All submissions must meet our testing and documentation requirements
- **Component Builder access required**: You must be an approved Component Builder to claim bounties
- **Example project required**: Your submission must include a working demonstration project
- **Original work only**: Components must be your own original creation

## Review Process

After you submit a claim:

1. **Initial review** (1-3 days): We test your component and example project
2. **Decision**: We'll email you with one of three outcomes:
   - ✅ **Accepted**: Congratulations! We'll arrange payment via PayPal or Venmo
   - ❌ **Rejected**: Component doesn't meet requirements (bounty remains available)
   - 🔄 **Changes requested**: Specific improvements needed before acceptance
3. **Payment**: Accepted bounties are paid within 5 business days
